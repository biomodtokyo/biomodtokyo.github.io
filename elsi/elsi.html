Ethical, Legal, and Social Issues (ELSI)

Overview

Our project acknowledges that scientific innovation must be accompanied by careful consideration of ethical, legal, and social implications (ELSI). We strive to identify potential risks early, protect participant privacy, minimize dual‑use concerns, and engage with stakeholders to ensure transparent, responsible research.

Our Commitments

  • Ethical oversight and IRB review when applicable
  • Clear informed consent and participant protections
  • Secure data governance and privacy safeguards
  • Risk assessment for dual‑use and mitigation measures
  • Open dialogue with stakeholders and public engagement
