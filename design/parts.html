DNA Sequences Used (List)

Below is the list of DNA strands used in this project. Sequences are written 5' → 3'. Modifications and phosphorothioate markers are indicated where applicable.

Legend / Notation

  • 5- ... -3: Sequence direction from 5' end to 3' end.
  • [FAM]: 5' fluorescent label (FAM).
  • *: Phosphorothioate-modified backbone at the preceding base (used in some bridge oligos).
  • -3'P: 3' phosphate blocking group.
