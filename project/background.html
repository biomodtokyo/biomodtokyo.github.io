Background

The Promise of DNA Nanotechnology

DNA is not just the blueprint of life; it's also a remarkable building material. Using the principles of Watson-Crick base pairing, we can program DNA strands to self-assemble into complex, custom-designed shapes on the nanoscale. This field, known as DNA origami, allows us to build everything from tiny containers to molecular-scale robots.
